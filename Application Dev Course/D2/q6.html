Video Attributes Used

  • controls: Displays play, pause, volume, and fullscreen controls
  • autoplay: Video starts playing automatically when page loads
  • loop: Video will loop/repeat continuously
  • muted: Video starts muted (user can unmute using controls)
  • width="100%": Makes the video responsive, taking full width of its container
